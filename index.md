# eDNA Student Hub — CRBE Toulouse

Welcome to the **eDNA Student Hub** page for the CRBE laboratory (Toulouse).

This space is **run by and for students** — PhD candidates and master's interns — working on environmental DNA, regardless of the environment studied (freshwater, soil, marine, microbiome...) or the stage involved (fieldwork, lab work, bioinformatics).

## What we do

Once a month, we meet to discuss a specific technical topic — a bioinformatics algorithm, a lab protocol step, a fieldwork question — to help move everyone's understanding forward.
